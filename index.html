Roll to victory!

  • Two players compete to reach 100 points first.
  • On your turn, roll the dice. Add the rolled number to your current score.
  • If you roll a 1, your turn ends, and you lose your current score.
  • You can choose to hold and save your current score, passing the turn to the other player.
  • Power-ups appear only once per player when their score reaches 30, and can give you an advantage or hinder your opponent!
